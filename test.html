About us

Beautiful Custom Cabinets Since 2014


Steve Moore Woodworks is in Gibson, TN and has been proudly serving the surrounding area for almost a decade.

We work with residential and commercial customers to create the custom cabinetry they have always dreamed of for any room.

Steve Moore and his team pride themselves in producing high quality all wood cabinets at competitive prices. Our products have a diverse offering of wood species and finishes. Blum hinges and drawer slides bring durable functionality to our pieces. We appreciate all our current and past customers and look forward to offering many more years of excellent design and quality construction.

Are you ready to get started? Check out our gallery to view some of our past projects and get design ideas. Do you already know what you want? Reach out for a design collaboration!
